About Us

Kansas-Based Hospitality

DHC of Kansas is the local ownership group behind Dave's Hot Chicken restaurants across the state.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve Kansas with exceptional hospitality.

We do this by building excellent restaurants, investing in our communities, and creating innumerable opportunities for our team and partners.

Excellent Restaurants

We are focused on building restaurants that deliver consistency, quality, and memorable guest experiences.

Community Investment

We want our restaurants to contribute to the places they call home through presence, partnership, and support.

Opportunity Creation

We believe strong hospitality businesses should create meaningful opportunities for team members and partners to grow.
