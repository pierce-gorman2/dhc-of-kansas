Who We Are

We are a Kansas-based Dave’s Hot Chicken franchise company, with a mission to create unforgettable guest experiences through exceptional food and unreasonable hospitality, while creating innumerable opportunities for our team and partners along the way.

About Dave's Hot Chicken

Dave's Hot Chicken started in 2017 when three childhood friends pooled $900 to open a pop-up in an East Hollywood parking lot. All they had was a portable fryer, folding tables, and a simple menu.

The concept gained traction quickly through Instagram, word-of-mouth, and a strong review from EATER LA. Before long, lines were forming around the block, leading them to open their first brick-and-mortar location in 2018.

Since then, the focus has remained the same: serve high-quality hot chicken with consistent flavor and execution that keeps people coming back. From that first location to now 400+ locations, the mission has stayed consistent - to blow every guest's mind with the best hot chicken in the world.
